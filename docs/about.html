Who built this?

My name is Colin Williams.

Why?

In the 2017 election, I had an extremely difficult time finding unbiased information on my candidates. I reasoned that, if I wanted a site like this other people would too.

How did you build this?

Are you tracking my personal information?

This site only presents data available to the general public. Voter registration status is a matter of public record, you too can get a complete list of all registered voters by emailing the city clerk.
